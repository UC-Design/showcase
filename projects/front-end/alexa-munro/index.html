Welcome to Churchill House

Churchill House, is located on 216 Northbourne Avenue in Braddon, Canberra, designed by Robin Boyd for the Winston Churchill Memorial Trust of Australia in 1969-70, with construction completed in 1971. It was Boyd’s last major commission, and he died prior to its completion. It is one of a small number of larger designs produced by Boyd and was Canberra’s first Brutalist building.

This website was produced by students in the Faculty of Arts & Design, University of Canberra, 2019.
