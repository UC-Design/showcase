History of KAVHA — Norfolk Island

Kingston and Arthurs Vale Historic Area

Welcome to the information page of KAVHA (Kingston and Arthurs Vale Historic Area). This website will provide you with an in-depth understanding of the historical significance of KAVHA and it's place on Norfolk Island. The history of KAVHA is rich and important to learn in order to understand the history of how Australia came to be and the positive and negative impacts of the early settlers.
