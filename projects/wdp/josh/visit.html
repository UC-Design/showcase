Visit

Tocumwal is in the southern Riverina region of New South Wales, Australia, in the Berrigan Shire local government area, near the Victorian border.

During November the aptly named festival 'Strawberry Fields' brings thousands together on the Murray river outside Tocumwal. Strawberries can be picked in the surrounding region year round, with the 'Big Strawberry' monument paying tribute to Tocumwal's fame for it's strawberries.

Piece's of Tocumwal's history remain, such as the bridge which was erected in 1895 and strengthened for the railway in 1905.
